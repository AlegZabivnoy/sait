Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Takeshi Yamamoto

With over 20 years of experience in Japanese haute cuisine, Chef Yamamoto brings his passion and expertise to Wai Kimono. Trained at the prestigious Kyoto Culinary Institute and having worked at renowned establishments in Tokyo and Kyoto, he is dedicated to preserving traditional techniques while introducing innovative flavors.

"Every dish tells a story. My goal is to share the rich culinary heritage of Japan through flavors that speak to the soul."
